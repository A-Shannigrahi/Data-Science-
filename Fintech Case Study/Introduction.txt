Title- App Behaviour Analysis

Objective- To predict which customers will not convert to paid membership so greater marketing efforts can go into converting them into paid users.

Case-The company has provided us the data of several customers who had downloaded the free version of the app.
      The data also mentions the current status of this customers (if they have converted to paid users or not).
      It is our task to analyse the behaviour of the customers and predict whether a particular user will enroll in the paid service option.
